4-Bar Linkage Motion Analysis

An analysis of the mechanical motion and torque in a four-bar linkage system, including simulation results and key performance indicators.

Project Overview

This project involved the simulation and analysis of a four-bar linkage mechanism to evaluate the motion, torque, and reaction forces involved in the system. The objective was to optimize motor torque and bearing forces for efficient motion. The analysis provided insights into the system's mechanical behavior under different loading conditions.

Results

  • Max Motor Torque: 654 N-mm (at near-vertical extension).
  • Max Reaction Force on Bearing: 14 N.
  • Max Reaction Force on New Bearing: 21 N.
  • Max Motor Power: 2233 W.

Technical Details

The linkage system was modeled and simulated in SolidWorks to calculate the torque, angular displacement, and reaction forces at various joints. The simulation included dynamic loading conditions to evaluate performance under realistic operating scenarios.
